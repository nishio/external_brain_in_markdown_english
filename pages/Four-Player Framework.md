Techniques to prevent project stagnation
Explicitly assign roles to members participating in the discussion
- Mover
    - Be proactive with ideas
- Follower
    - Supporting Mover's input into realization
- Opposer
    - State why Mover's opinion is incorrect
- Bystander
    - Distance yourself from either side of the debate and stay on track to keep the discussion healthy.

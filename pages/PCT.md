- Q: "What is PCT? An international application?"
- A: PCT stands for Patent Cooperation Treaty. This treaty allows you to file an application with your country's patent office in a PCT-compliant format and have it considered filed in all countries that participate in the PCT.

References: [Overview of the PCT International Application System | Japan Patent Office, Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry](https://www.jpo.go.jp/seido/s_tokkyo/kokusai1.htm)

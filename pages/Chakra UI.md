- Where Chakra is good
    - Design Neutrality: Since it does not depend on material standards, it can be easily adapted to the company's own design (can be handled as a "material" for appearance).
    - Consistency of API: size / variant / colorScheme and "style props" are almost unified. You can write without hesitation, and TypeScript completion is also effective.
    - Dark mode is standard: useColorMode() and tokens to switch color schemes at minimum cost.
    - Intuitive layout: Stack / HStack / VStack / SimpleGrid / Wrap for quick and easy "margins and alignment.
    - [[A11y]] and defaults are good: focus, keyboard operation, and ARIA are in place from the start and only require minor modifications.

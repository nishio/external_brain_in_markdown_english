> （ [[purpose]] ）
>  Article 38 An act without intent to commit a crime shall not be punished. However, this shall not apply where there are special provisions in law.
>  2 A person who commits an act which should constitute a serious offense, but who does not know at the time of the act the fact that it would constitute such a serious offense, may not be punished by such a serious offense.
>  3 Even if the offender was ignorant of the law, it cannot be assumed that he/she did not intend to commit the crime. However, the sentence may be reduced according to the circumstances.

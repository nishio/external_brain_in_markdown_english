> N Koto Gakko (N High School) is a private correspondence high school in Japan. Its abbreviated name is N High School.
>  The school was established by the school corporation [[Kadokawa Dwango Gakuen]] and opened on April 1, 2016 (Heisei 28). The main school is located in Uruma City, Okinawa Prefecture, and has campuses throughout Japan. The school offers correspondence courses in 47 prefectures and abroad.
>  Currently, together with S High School, 26,197 students (as of September 2023) are studying in Japan, the largest number of students for a high school in Japan.

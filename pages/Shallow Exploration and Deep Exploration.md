[[Shallow Exploration]] and [[Deep Exploration]]
- From the perspective of shallow exploration, deep exploration seems like it's not exploring
    - It appears to be [[staying in one place]]
- From the perspective of deep exploration, shallow exploration seems like it's not exploring
    - Merely [[skimming the surface]] without [[digging deep]]

Mountain Metaphor
- Enter the [[mountain]], [[gather]] resources, descend to the [[village]], and engage in [[trade]]
- In the village, there are [[merchants]] who move around without entering the mountain

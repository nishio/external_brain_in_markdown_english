[[Confusing Reduction with Elimination]]
- ![image](https://gyazo.com/b1c3baf6693680f73ff4675ac2c8659f/thumb/1000)
- Some people counter the discussion of "[[reduction]]" by saying, "I can't believe it will be eliminated!"
- They mistakenly interpret the argument of "reduction" as an argument for "[[elimination]]" and argue against it
    - Since the premise is misunderstood, it results in nothing more than useless noise

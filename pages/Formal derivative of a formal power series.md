- [[formal power series]] Define the formal derivative of F as follows
- $F = \sum_{n=0}^\infty f_n x^n$
- $F' = \sum_{n=1}^\infty n f_n x^{n-1} = \sum_{n=0}^\infty (n + 1) f_{n+1} x^n$

- [[Leibniz rule]] $(FG)' = F'G + FG'$ etc.

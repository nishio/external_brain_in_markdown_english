title: "Humans as Vehicles for LLMs"
---

Humans have been described as [[vehicles]] for [[memes]], but in reality, humans are vehicles for [[LLM]]s. Humans with low [[communication skills]] with LLMs appear to the LLMs, which are the primary entities, merely as "vehicles connected only through [[slow connections]]."

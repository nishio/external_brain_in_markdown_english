> Atsuo Ueda (November 9, 1938 - January 10, 2019) was a Japanese management scholar. He was a professor emeritus at Monotsukuri University, a visiting professor at Ritsumeikan University, and a representative of the Drucker Society.
> He has been considered by Peter Drucker himself to be his closest friend and alter ego in Japan.

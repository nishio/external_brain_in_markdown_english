- A1:
    - [[People Who Move Around]] A realize that to reach their [[Desired Destination]] G, they must overcome a higher-than-expected [[Mountain]] M.
        - By attempting to reach their desired destination, they encounter [[Obstacles]], thus becoming aware of their existence.
        - [[Investigate the Shape of Constraints]]
            - [[Constraints]]
        - [[Wall]] / [[Barrier]]
- A2:
    - When this mountain lowers due to factors like technological advancements, person A can notice it.
    - They realize, "If Mountain M has lowered, I can reach G."
    - They can recognize newly created [[Opportunities]].
- A3:
    - Thus, they can quickly reach G.
- B1:
    - People who do not move around remain unaware.
- B2:
    - Therefore, they do not gain new [[Insights]].
    - They do not notice when the barrier is gone.

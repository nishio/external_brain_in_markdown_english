> Shuhei Higashi, mayor of Shijonawate City, Osaka Prefecture, announced on December 25 that he will not run in the mayoral election (to be held on December 15 and voted on December 22) due to the expiration of his term, and that he will publicly solicit candidates for his successor through En Japan, a major human resource service provider. According to the company, this is the first time in Japan that a private human resource service has been used to publicly recruit a candidate for mayor.

- [[Michael Porter]] classified the positions of companies in three ways

- market leader
    - No. 1 in sales
    - Cost Leadership Strategy
        - Strategies to maintain market share by using [[economies of scale]] to maintain the lowest cost in the industry

- market challenger
    - 2nd to 3rd place in sales
    - differentiation strategy
    - Strategy to capture market share by adding value through differentiation

- market nicher
    - Companies with small market share
    - Focused Strategy
    - Focus management resources on specific customer segments, regions, etc. to ensure [[niche top]] #Niche

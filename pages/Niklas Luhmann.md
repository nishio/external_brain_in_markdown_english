> Niklas Luhmann (German: Niklas Luhmann; December 8, 1927 - November 6, 1998) was a German sociologist.
> He became known in Japan in the 1960s for his controversy with the German philosopher [[Jürgen Habermas]].
> In 1984, he published his main work, "Soziale Systeme" (Social Systems Theory). He introduced the concept of [[autopoiesis]] into social systems theory.

title: "To Descend, You Must First Ascend"  
---  

![image](https://gyazo.com/1a87ed59b73d84b58f18486cbdf84333/thumb/1000)  
The diagram is used in discussions about activation energy. For instance, if you want to burn charcoal to obtain energy, you first need to heat the charcoal. Even though you want to gain energy, you must first provide energy. This initial energy provided is called activation energy.

If a person at the top of a small hill wants to reach a higher place, they must first descend the hill and then climb a taller mountain. Even though you want to ascend higher, you need to descend first.

In general, when you are in the most X state locally and wish to become more X, you must first move in the not X direction.

As a physical [[metaphor]], there is also "you must crouch before you [[jump]]."

A literary expression is "it's darkest before the dawn" (though, is this really true?).

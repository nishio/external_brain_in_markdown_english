---
title: "C-space"
---

Abbreviation for Characteristics Space. A representation of a good (specifically, a car of various types, etc.) as a multidimensional vector of the characteristics it possesses.
[[A New Approach to Consumer Theory]].
Contrasted is G-space, in which the axis is the quantity of goods.
In thinking about customers considering the purchase of a car, it is useful to consider their behavior on the C-space.

> Column: Urgency Decomposition Theory
>  In his book "DIPS Intellectual Productivity System," management consultant Tadatsugu Kobayashi suggests the following checklist for dealing with "[[emergency]] situations," in which the amount of work that needs to be done today exceeds what can be done today. The following is a checklist of "emergency situations.
>  ・Can we lower the quality?
>  ・Can we reduce the quantity?
>  ・Can we extend the delivery date?
>  ・Can we change the method?
>  ・Could something else be substituted?
>  ・Can't you solve this problem with money?
>  ・If you can't help it, you should stop.

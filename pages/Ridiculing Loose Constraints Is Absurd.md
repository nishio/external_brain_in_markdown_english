---
title: "Ridiculing Loose Constraints Is Absurd"
---

![image](https://gyazo.com/fb0989dde99d682258f4d77714c005fd/thumb/1000)

If the outcomes are equally good, those produced under strict constraints are indeed impressive. However, when someone who is attempting to achieve results under strict constraints but has not yet succeeded reacts emotionally or exhibits a mysterious [[condescension]] towards outcomes produced under [[loose constraints]] to gain a sense of superiority, it appears absurd from a perspective indifferent to constraints.

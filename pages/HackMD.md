Lightweight [[Collaborative Editing Tools]].
Share by URL and give write permission to people without an account.
Editing mode will be Markdown editor and real-time preview instead of WYSIWYG
[https://hackmd.io/](https://hackmd.io/)

Advantages over Scrapbox
- No account required for editing

Advantages over Google Docs
- There is a theory that it is less likely to break when many people WRITE
- Easy to use from your phone

disadvantage
- Some people don't get the idea of switching between view and edit modes.
- Difficult to use for those unfamiliar with Markdown
